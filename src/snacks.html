SAMOSA


Samosa

Ingredients:
For the dough
2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons oil, Water (as needed)
For the filling
2 medium potatoes(boiled, peeled, and mashed), 1/2 cup green peas(boiled and mashed), 1 small onion(finely chopped), 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste, 1 green chili(finely chopped) , 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon garam masala, Salt to taste, 2 tablespoons oil
For frying
Sufficient oil for deep frying
Instructions:

  • -- Start by preparing the dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, salt, and oil. Mix well until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Gradually add water and knead the mixture into a smooth, firm dough. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.
  • -- While the dough is resting, let's make the filling. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter. Then add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • -- Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili (if using), and sauté for a minute. Then add the mashed potatoes and green peas. Mix well.
  • -- Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir everything together and cook for a few minutes until the flavors blend well. Remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool.
  • -- Once the dough has rested, divide it into small lemon-sized balls. Take one ball and roll it out into a small circle of about 6-7 inches in diameter. Cut this circle in half.
  • -- Take one semicircle and fold it into a cone shape, overlapping the straight edges. Seal the edges by applying a little water. Fill the cone with a tablespoon or two of the prepared filling. Again, apply water to the open edges and press to seal the samosa.
  • -- Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling.
  • -- Heat oil for deep frying in a pan or kadai over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully slide in a few samosas at a time and fry them until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove them using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  • -- Serve the samosas hot with mint chutney, tamarind chutney, or ketchup.

MIRCHI BAJJI


Mirchi Bajji

Ingredients:
8-10 large green chilies (such as banana peppers or jalapenos),1 cup besan (gram flour),1/4 cup rice flour,1 teaspoon red chili powder,1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder,1/2 teaspoon cumin powder,1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain),Salt to taste,Water (as needed for the batter),Oil for deep frying
For the Stuffing
2 medium-sized potatoes(boiled and mashed),1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds,1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds,1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder,1/2 teaspoon red chili powder,Salt to taste,1 tablespoon oil
Instructions:

  • -- Wash the green chilies and pat them dry. Make a vertical slit on each chili, ensuring not to cut them into two separate pieces. Remove the seeds if you prefer a milder taste.
  • -- In a pan, heat oil for the stuffing. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • -- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to the pan. Mix well.
  • -- Add the mashed potatoes and combine everything thoroughly. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  • -- Stuff each chili with the prepared potato filling. Press gently to seal the chilies.
  • -- In a mixing bowl, combine besan, rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, carom seeds, and salt. Mix well.
  • -- Gradually add water to the dry ingredients while stirring continuously to form a smooth batter. The batter should have a slightly thick consistency.
  • -- Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai for deep frying over medium heat.
  • -- Dip each stuffed chili into the prepared batter, ensuring it is coated evenly. Gently drop the coated chilies into the hot oil.
  • -- Fry the chilies until they turn golden brown and crisp. Flip them occasionally for even frying. Remove the fried chilies using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  • -- Repeat the process with the remaining stuffed chilies and batter.
  • -- Serve the hot Mirchi Bajjis with mint chutney or tomato ketchup.

PAKODI


Pakodi

Ingredients:
2 large onions(thinly sliced),1 cup gram flour (besan),2 tablespoons rice flour,1 teaspoon red chili powder,1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder,1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds,A pinch of asafoetida (hing),Salt to taste,Water (as needed for the batter),Oil for deep frying
Instructions:

  • -- In a large mixing bowl, add the thinly sliced onions.
  • -- Add gram flour, rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and salt to the bowl.
  • -- Mix all the ingredients together, ensuring that the onion slices are well coated with the flour mixture. The moisture from the onions will help in binding the mixture.
  • -- Gradually add water to the mixture while stirring continuously. Add water in small quantities to achieve a thick batter consistency.
  • -- Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai for deep frying over medium heat.
  • -- Once the oil is hot, drop small portions of the batter into the oil using your fingers or a spoon. You can shape the pakodis into small round fritters or irregular shapes as desired.
  • -- Fry the pakodis until they turn golden brown and crispy. Flip them occasionally for even frying. Fry them in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  • -- Remove the fried pakodis using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  • -- Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • -- Serve the hot Onion Pakodis with mint chutney or tomato ketchup.

NOODELS


Noodels

Ingredients:
8 ounces (225 grams) noodles (such as egg noodles, rice noodles, or spaghetti),2 tablespoons oil,1 tablespoon minced garlic,1 tablespoon minced ginger,1 cup mixed vegetables (such as sliced carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, mushrooms, or snow peas),2 tablespoons soy sauce,1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste),1 tablespoon vinegar,1 teaspoon sugar,Salt to taste,Optional toppings: chopped green onions, cilantro (coriander leaves), or toasted sesame seeds
Instructions:

  • -- Cook the noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • -- Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  • -- Add minced garlic and minced ginger to the hot oil. Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  • -- Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
  • -- In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  • -- Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and pour the sauce mixture into the empty space.
  • -- Stir the sauce mixture for a few seconds until it starts to bubble.
  • -- Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss them with the sauce and vegetables until everything is well coated.
  • -- Continue to stir-fry the noodles for a few minutes until they are heated through and the flavors are well combined.
  • -- Taste and adjust the seasoning according to your preference by adding more soy sauce, chili sauce, or salt if needed.
  • -- Remove the pan from heat and garnish the noodles with chopped green onions, cilantro, or toasted sesame seeds if desired.
  • -- Serve the Veg Noodles hot and enjoy!

PAV BAJJI


Pav Bajji

Ingredients:
2 large potatoes(boiled and mashed),1 cup mixed vegetables (such as cauliflower, carrots, peas, bell peppers), finely chopped,1 large onion(finely chopped),3-4 garlic cloves(minced),1-inch ginger(grated),2 tomatoes, finely chopped,2 tablespoons butter,2 tablespoons Pav Bhaji masala,1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to taste),1 tablespoon lemon juice,Salt to taste,Pav (buns) for serving,Finely chopped onions and coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

  • -- Heat butter in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  • -- Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • -- Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell goes away.
  • -- Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  • -- Add mixed vegetables and mix well. Cook for 5-6 minutes until the vegetables are partially cooked.
  • -- Add boiled and mashed potatoes to the pan. Mix everything together.
  • -- Add Pav Bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • -- Using a potato masher or the back of a spoon, mash the vegetables and potatoes together until well combined.
  • -- Add some water to adjust the consistency and simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • -- Add lemon juice and mix well. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  • -- Toast the pav (buns) with butter on a griddle or pan until they turn golden brown.
  • -- Serve the hot Pav Bhaji with buttered pav, garnished with finely chopped onions and coriander leaves.

BURGER


Burger

Ingredients:
For the patty
1 cup cooked and mashed vegetables (such as potatoes, carrots, peas, corn),1/2 cup bread crumbs,2 tablespoons finely chopped onions,1 tablespoon finely chopped bell peppers (capsicum),1 teaspoon minced garlic,1 teaspoon minced ginger,1 teaspoon soy sauce,1/2 teaspoon cumin powder,1/2 teaspoon paprika or red chili powder,Salt to taste,Oil for cooking
For assembling the burger
Burger buns,Lettuce leaves,Sliced tomatoes,Sliced onions,Sliced cheese (optional),Condiments of your choice (ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard, etc.)
Instructions:

  • -- In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed vegetables, bread crumbs, finely chopped onions, finely chopped bell peppers, minced garlic, minced ginger, soy sauce, cumin powder, paprika or red chili powder, and salt. Mix well until everything is evenly combined.
  • -- Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into patties of your desired size.
  • -- Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, place the patties in the pan and cook them for about 3-4 minutes on each side until they turn golden brown and crispy. You can also grill or bake the patties if desired.
  • -- Once the patties are cooked, remove them from the pan and set them aside.
  • -- Slice the burger buns in half horizontally. Toast them lightly if desired.
  • -- Spread your choice of condiments (ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard) on the bottom half of the bun.
  • -- Place a lettuce leaf on top of the condiments.
  • -- Put the cooked patty on top of the lettuce.
  • -- Layer sliced tomatoes and onions over the patty.
  • -- If using cheese, place a slice of cheese on top of the vegetables.
  • -- Cover the burger with the top half of the bun.
  • -- Serve the veg burger immediately and enjoy!

PIZZA


Pizza

Ingredients:
For the pizza dough
2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast,1 teaspoon sugar,1 ½ cups warm water,3 ½ cups all-purpose flour,2 tablespoons olive oil,1 ½ teaspoons salt
For the pizza sauce
1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes,2 tablespoons tomato paste,2 cloves garlic, minced,1 teaspoon dried basil,1 teaspoon dried oregano,1 tablespoon olive oil,Salt and pepper to taste
For Toppings
Mozzarella cheese (shredded or fresh slices),Your choice of toppings such as sliced pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, sliced onions, sliced mushrooms, etc.
Instructions:
1.Preapare the dough

  • -- In a small bowl, combine the active dry yeast, sugar, and warm water. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  • -- In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  • -- Stir the mixture with a spoon until it comes together, then transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  • -- Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  • 2.Prepare the pizza sauce
  • -- In a small sauce pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  • -- Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  • -- Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens. Remove from heat and set aside.
  • 3.Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to preheat as well.
  • 4.Shape and assemble the pizza
  • -- Punch down the risen pizza dough and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out one portion of the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and size.
  • -- Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough onto a piece of parchment paper.
  • -- Spoon a generous amount of the pizza sauce onto the dough and spread it evenly, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  • -- Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your desired toppings.
  • -- Repeat the process with the second portion of dough or save it for later use.
  • 5.Bake the pizza.
  • -- Carefully slide the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  • -- Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  • -- Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

VEG MANCHURIA


Veg Manchuria

Ingredients:
For the Manchurian balls
1 cup grated cabbage,1/2 cup grated carrot,1/2 cup finely chopped bell peppers (capsicum),1/4 cup finely chopped onions,1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida),1/4 cup corn flour,2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro (coriander leaves),2 teaspoons minced ginger,2 teaspoons minced garlic,1 teaspoon soy sauce,1 teaspoon vinegar,1/2 teaspoon red chili powder,Salt to taste,Oil for deep frying
For the Manchurian sauce
2 tablespoons oil,1 tablespoon minced garlic,1 tablespoon minced ginger,1/4 cup finely chopped onions,1/4 cup finely chopped bell peppers (capsicum),2 tablespoons tomato ketchup,1 tablespoon soy sauce,1 tablespoon vinegar,1 teaspoon red chili sauce (adjust to taste),1 teaspoon corn flour mixed with 2 tablespoons water (slurry),1 cup water,Salt to taste,Chopped cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish
Instructions:

  • -- Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  • -- Add minced garlic and minced ginger. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  • -- Add chopped onions and bell peppers. Stir-fry for a couple of minutes until they turn slightly tender.
  • -- Add tomato ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, red chili sauce, and salt. Mix well.
  • -- In a separate bowl, mix corn flour with water to form a slurry. Add the slurry to the pan and mix it into the sauce.
  • -- Add water and bring the sauce to a simmer. Cook for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  • -- Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and toss gently to coat them evenly with the sauce.
  • -- Cook for another minute or two, allowing the Manchurian balls to absorb the flavors of the sauce.
  • -- Garnish with chopped cilantro.
